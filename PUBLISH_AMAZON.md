## Step 2: Create Amazon Developer Account (FREE)
1. Go to: https://developer.amazon.com
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Select: "I want to publish Android apps"
4. Fill:
   - Developer Name: (your name or company)
   - Email: (your email)
   - Phone: (your phone number)
5. Verify email
6. **No credit card needed!**

---

## Step 3: Submit App to Amazon
