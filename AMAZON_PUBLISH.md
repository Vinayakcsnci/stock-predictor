# Amazon Appstore Publishing Guide

## Step 1: Create Developer Account (FREE)
1. Go to: https://developer.amazon.com
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Choose "I want to publish Android apps"
4. Fill in your details (name, email, phone)
5. Verify email
6. **No credit card required!**

---

## Step 2: Convert Web App to APK
1. Go to: https://website2apk.com
2. Enter URL: `https://stock-predictor-8tvwfmtbkhszqffnsvpo4f.streamlit.app`
3. App Name: **Stock Price Predictor**
4. Package Name: `com.stockpredictor.app`
5. Click **Convert to APK**
6. Download the APK

---

## Step 3: Submit to Amazon Appstore
1. Go to: https://developer.amazon.com
2. Login
3. Click **Apps & Services** â†’ **My Apps**
4. Click **Add a New App**
5. Select **Android**
6. Fill:
   - **App Title**: Stock Price Predictor
   - **Category**: Finance
   - **Subcategory**: Stock Market

### App Files Tab:
- Upload your APK

### Descriptions Tab:
- **Short Description**: Predict next day's stock price
- **Long Description**: 
```
Stock Price Predictor uses Machine Learning to predict next day's closing price. Simply enter any stock ticker symbol (like AAPL, GOOGL, TSLA) and get instant AI-powered predictions.

Features:
- Real-time stock data from Yahoo Finance
- ML-based price prediction
- Interactive price charts
- Works with any ticker symbol

Disclaimer: This app is for educational purposes only. Stock predictions are not financial advice.
```
